Cash App helps you track your spendings, start saving and prepares you for the future

Confused as to how to start?

Thats where we come in. Read the guidelines below and you are good to go!

Merchants! 🛒

  • Have your favourite shops ready and on the go! You can add your favourite online stores,
    supermarkets, car retail and even cinemas saved!
  • These will come in hand when making your transactions.

Tags 🚆

  • Sort your transactions into categories. You can add tags like shopping, groceries and transportation.
  • With our special algorithm, you can check your transactions for each tag, and how much you have spent.

Transactions 🧾

  • Using your merchants and tags, you can create new transactions and add amounts as
    well as dates for each transaction.
  • With our special in built function, you can compare the amount spent with respect
    to your total budget, and this can give you a breif idea as to what your spending is like.
